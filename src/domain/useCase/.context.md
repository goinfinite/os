## Constraints

- MUST encapsulate business rules and validation
- MUST NOT know about HTTP, CLI, or storage implementation details
- MUST communicate through DTOs when crossing layer boundaries

## Guidance

- SHOULD keep use cases focused on a single business operation
- SHOULD delegate to repositories for persistence
- SHOULD raise domain errors (not infrastructure errors) for business rule violations
